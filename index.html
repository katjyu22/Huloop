HULOOP

Huloop is a new calendaring feature on Hulu that allows you to schedule watch parties with your loved ones. Huloop syncs with your calendar and creates a personalized event to share with your family and friends.

SCHEDULE WITH HULOOP TODAY

*Available for subscribers on the following Hulu Plans: Hulu (no Ads) or Hulu (no Ads) + Live TV*

Get started.

How to Huloop.

  • 1. FIND HULOOP IN THE TOP NAVIGATION OR IN THE DETAILS PAGE OF A VIDEO.

  • 2. SYNC YOUR GOOGLE, OUTLOOK, OR APPLE CALENDAR.

  • 3. SELECT YOUR AVAILABLE DATES AND TIMES.

  • 4. INVITE GUESTS OR SEND OUT A HYPERLINK TO WATCH THE PARTY.

  • 5. JOIN THE HULU WATCH PARTY!
